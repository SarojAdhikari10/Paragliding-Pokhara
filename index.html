About us

Local team, unforgettable experiences

Paragliding Pokhara Nepal is a professional adventure company based in Lakeside, Pokhara. Founder Binod Gautam holds a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and has more than 20 years of experience in Nepal's tourism and adventure industry. He leads the business with a strong focus on safety, customer support and memorable travel experiences.

Saroj Adhikari supports the business through website management, customer communication, digital presence and operational assistance while pursuing postgraduate research in Artificial Intelligence in Australia.
